Frequently Asked Questions



Q. Will the teacher be able to edit the result?

Ans. Yes, the teacher has the permission to edit the result.


Q. How to Login as Student?

Ans. Click the Dropdown list then select student, and enter te username password.


Q. How to add new course from teachers portals?

Ans.Click on Course then click on add course in teachers portal.


Q. Can the Students correct their login Details?

Ans. Yes, the student is required to send a mail to the admin for updating their Login Details.


Q. Can each student view every students result?

Ans. No, each student can only view their own result on their portal.
